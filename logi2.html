Our Services

Our comprehensive range of logistics services is designed to meet your specific needs and exceed your expectations.

Air Freight

  • Express Shipping: Rapid delivery for urgent shipments.
  • Consolidated Air Freight: Cost-effective option for multiple consignments.
  • Charter Flights: Customized solutions for large or oversized shipments.

Ocean Freight

  • Full Container Load (FCL): Dedicated container for your exclusive use.
  • Less Than Container Load (LCL): Shared container for smaller shipments.
  • Breakbulk: Shipping of oversized or heavy cargo.

Ground Transportation

  • Truckload: Dedicated truck for your entire shipment.
  • Less Than Truckload (LTL): Shared truck for smaller shipments.
  • Intermodal Transportation: Combination of rail and truck for long-distance shipments.

Custom Brokerage

  • Import and Export Clearance: Expert assistance with customs procedures.
  • Documentation: Preparation and submission of required customs documents.
  • Compliance: Adherence to import and export regulations.

Supply Chain Management

  • Inventory Management: Optimization of inventory levels.
  • Warehouse Management: Efficient storage and handling of goods.
  • Transportation Management: Coordination of all transportation activities.

Additional Services

  • Insurance: Comprehensive coverage for your shipments.
  • Packaging: Custom packaging solutions to protect your goods.
  • Hazardous Materials Handling: Safe transportation of hazardous substances.

No matter the size or complexity of your shipment, Xpress International is committed to providing reliable, efficient, and cost-effective logistics solutions.
