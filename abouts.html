Founded in 2016, Bangladeshi is a family-owned business dedicated to preserving and sharing the culinary heritage of Bengal food . Inspired by a deep love for our country's rich food traditions, we set out to bring the authentic flavors of Bengali cuisine to homes and kitchens around the world.

Our Commitment

At the heart of our business is a commitment to quality, sustainability, and authenticity. We source our fish and rice directly from local farmers and fishermen in Bangladesh, ensuring that our products are fresh, flavorful, and support the livelihoods of local communities. We adhere to strict quality control standards to guarantee that every product meets our high expectations. Our team of experts carefully selects and processes each item, ensuring it arrives at your doorstep in optimal condition.

Our Values

  • Sustainability:We prioritize sustainable practices to protect the environment and support local communities.
  • Authenticity:We are dedicated to preserving the traditional flavors and cooking methods of Bengali cuisine.
  • Quality:We strive to provide the highest quality products and exceptional customer service.
  • Community:We are committed to supporting local farmers, fishermen, and communities in Bangladesh and West Bengal.

Experience the Taste of Bengal & Bangladesh

Whether you're a seasoned Bengali cook or simply looking to explore new flavors, Bangladeshi Fresh offers a unique opportunity to experience the authentic tastes of Bangladesh. Join us on a culinary journey and discover the rich, diverse, and delicious world of Bengali cuisine.
